Why I Made This

I didn’t start this as a project. I started it because I was breaking inside. I didn’t trust people. I didn’t know how to talk. I didn’t even believe my feelings mattered.

But with time, I realized something — Maybe I’m not the only one feeling this. Maybe there are thousands like me. Quiet. Creative. Sensitive. Tired of holding everything alone.

I didn’t create this to fix people. I made it so someone — even one person — could feel heard in a world that often doesn’t care. This space is not perfect. But it’s honest. And that’s enough for now.

“Some of us don’t need answers. We just need someone who won’t walk away.”

If this space helped you — even a little — I’m glad you're here. You're not alone, even if it feels like it sometimes.
